Astronaut Suit Design for Asteriods, Inc

Starting to work with a new astronaut suit design for the characters in Asteriods, Inc.

The idea is to account for the following:

  • Life support / Oxygen
  • Tools for fixing mining equipment
  • Propulsion / Jet pack for traveling around an asteriod
  • Display of suit status

Since its a comic the realism doesn’t have to be crucial but its nice to work in some functional aspects of what is really needed. I’m working on the overall ship design incorporating some real science. I’ll post some sketches as they come along.
